WHAT WE DO !

There are many genuine job seekers and test takers playing by the rules of demonstrating legitimate knowledge as either explicitly specified or implied which are being gained by some fraudulent jobseekers and students who tricked voice-based exams and are seeking the jobs and getting good grades, respectively. Our application will be able to identify those illicit participants who are using real-time expert proxies during the job interview that are providing the answers on their behalf. Artificial intelligence will help those conducting interviews or exams to detect such fraudulent candidates. This AI model is called AIPFD which stands for Artificial intelligence Proxy Fraud Detector. This model is developed to detect whether the participant’s voice is genuine or whether it is a voice of the expert proxy speaking from another location on the behalf of the fraudulent participant (job candidate, student in the oral exam etc.), with their voice being conducted through the participant’s phone speaker. AI PFD is also capable of detecting the genuine voice or fraud voice irrespective of the gender, language or the device used by the fraudulent participant and it uses the discrepancies in audio sample frequency patterns to detect whether the voice is originating from the participant or the illegal proxy via phone speaker.
